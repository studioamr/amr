# SUBSUELO — 8 rolas · cero voces · Boiler Room

**AMR · 123 BPM · óxido sobre concreto**

---

## EL CONCEPTO

Un Boiler Room no es un espectáculo. Es un cuarto, gente apretada, el DJ al ras
del piso, sin escenario y sin luces. La música no acompaña a nada — **es lo único
que está pasando.** Por eso este disco no tiene voces: nadie está hablando ahí.

**SUBSUELO son ocho niveles hacia abajo.** Empieza en la banqueta, con el bajo
saliéndose por una puerta, y va bajando hasta la roca sobre la que está parada la
ciudad. En BASALTO toca fondo. Después el agua se filtra de vuelta y en la última
se abre una puerta y entra luz.

Es lo contrario de [OFRENDA](ALBUM-OFRENDA.md): allá eran fuerzas naturales y el
mundo contestaba. Aquí no contesta nadie. Es concreto, fierro y groove.

## LA REGLA QUE NO SE ROMPE

**CERO VOCES.** Ni cantadas, ni picadas, ni respiraciones, ni vocales, ni coros,
ni "aahs". Nada. Es el pedido literal de André y va en los 8 prompts, escrito de
cuatro formas distintas porque una sola no basta para que Gemini lo respete.

## LA PALETA

**Óxido `#B4501F` sobre concreto `#2A2724`**, con blanco de cal `#D8D2C6`.

El color del fierro cuando se moja: varilla expuesta, tapa de alcantarilla, luz
de sodio en un estacionamiento. Caliente pero sucio — nada de neón.

Contra el ámbar, el vino, el azul, el jade, el violeta y la ceniza del catálogo,
este es **el disco industrial.**

---

## ⚠️ LO QUE APRENDIMOS CON AURORA

AURORA salió sonando a Avicii y **la culpa fue del prompt**: le pedí a Gemini
*"bright, vast, transcendent"*, *"the peak of the record"* y tonalidad **mayor**.
Esa es la receta exacta del EDM de estadio — no hay que sorprenderse de que la
haya seguido.

Conclusión que se aplica en los 8 prompts de abajo: **no basta con no pedir lo
malo, hay que prohibirlo.** Todos traen un bloque `NEVER` explícito.

## LOS 8 NIVELES

### 01 · ACERA — el bajo saliéndose por una puerta
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, A minor. Title: ACERA.
NO VOCALS. This track contains no voices of any kind — no singing, no chopped vocal samples, no choir, no humming, no breaths, no spoken word, no wordless vowels. Purely instrumental. If a vocal appears, the track is wrong.
MOOD: standing on the pavement outside a basement club at 1am. You can only hear the low end coming through a closed door. Muffled, patient, functional.
GROOVE: soft rounded kick, four on the floor, never harsh. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD INSTRUMENT — warm analog bass, rubbery and funky, rolling in the pocket with the kick, moving through a filter that opens slowly. Never distorted, never aggressive.
PERCUSSION: dry hand percussion, shakers, a cowbell answering off the beat. Recorded in a real room with air around it.
HARMONY: almost none. One dark minor stab, short and dry, and a semitone of tension underneath that never resolves.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 minutes. Constant groove, zero ceremony. It starts already moving and keeps moving.
NEVER: no euphoric major-key chord swells, no supersaw leads, no festival build-ups, no risers, no big drop, no EDM. Nothing anthemic.
MIX: warm, mid focused, slightly muffled like it's heard through a wall. Keep the top end restrained and dark.
```

### 02 · ESCALERA — bajando
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, A minor. Title: ESCALERA.
NO VOCALS. No voices of any kind — no singing, no vocal chops, no choir, no breaths, no wordless vowels. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: going down the stairs. Each step the low end gets closer and the muffling comes off. Anticipation without release.
GROOVE: soft kick, four on the floor. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD — warm analog bass, funky and syncopated, driving the whole track. A resonant filter opens gradually across the six minutes so the track gets brighter and closer as it goes.
PERCUSSION: congas and shakers weaving between the kicks, a woodblock, hand claps low in the mix. Human and loose.
HARMONY: one repeated minor stab. Semitone tension.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 minutes. One long uninterrupted opening — the filter IS the arrangement. No sections, no drop.
NEVER: no euphoric chords, no supersaw, no build-and-drop structure, no risers, no anthemic melody.
MIX: warm and mid focused. Keep the top end restrained and slightly dark.
```

### 03 · CONCRETO — ya estás adentro
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, F minor. Title: CONCRETO.
NO VOCALS. No voices, no vocal samples, no choir, no humming, no breaths. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: inside the room now. Packed, hot, no stage, no lights. Just the machine working.
GROOVE: soft rounded kick. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD — warm analog bass, heavy and rubbery, the most physical thing in the mix. Rolling and relentless.
PERCUSSION: dense hand percussion, cowbell, rim, shakers, everything dry and close. This is where the percussion is thickest.
HARMONY: a single dark minor stab and a semitone rub underneath. Nothing melodic.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 minutes. Constant groove, zero ceremony, no ornament. Functional club music.
NEVER: no euphoric major chords, no supersaw leads, no builds, no drops, no risers, nothing anthemic or emotional.
MIX: dry, close, warm. Keep the top end restrained and dark.
```

### 04 · DUCTO — el tiro de aire
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, F minor. Title: DUCTO.
NO VOCALS. No voices of any kind. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: a vertical air shaft. Everything echoes upward and comes back late. Narrow, metallic, hypnotic.
GROOVE: soft kick. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD — warm analog bass with a slow filter LFO, hypnotic and repetitive.
PERCUSSION: metallic percussion with long delay throwing hits up and back — a delay that repeats in time with the groove and slowly feeds back. Dry hand percussion underneath keeps it grounded.
HARMONY: one metallic stab drowned in delay. Semitone tension.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 minutes. The delay is the melody. Hypnotic repetition, no development.
NEVER: no euphoric chords, no supersaw, no builds, no drops, no anthemic melody.
MIX: narrow and metallic with deep space behind it. Keep the top end restrained and dark.
```

### 05 · CIMIENTO — los cimientos
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, C minor. Title: CIMIENTO.
NO VOCALS. No voices of any kind. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: down at the foundations. The weight of everything above pressing down. Slow-burning and heavy without being loud.
GROOVE: soft deep kick. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD — the lowest and most sustained bass on the record, warm and analog, holding long notes under the groove. Never distorted.
PERCUSSION: sparse and deliberate. Fewer elements than the tracks before it, each one landing hard.
HARMONY: one long dark minor tone, unresolved.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 minutes. Patient and heavy. Space between the hits.
NEVER: no euphoric chords, no supersaw, no builds, no drops, no risers, nothing bright.
MIX: deep, warm, spacious. Keep the top end restrained and dark.
```

### 06 · BASALTO — el fondo. La más pesada.
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, C minor. Title: BASALTO.
NO VOCALS. No voices of any kind. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: bedrock. The volcanic rock the whole city sits on. This is the deepest and heaviest point of the record — but heavy means DENSE and RELENTLESS, not loud or euphoric.
GROOVE: soft kick that hits like a weight. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD — thick warm analog bass, acid-tinged, squelching through a resonant filter that opens and closes across the track. Funky, never distorted.
PERCUSSION: full and driving, cowbell, congas, toms. Everything rolling.
HARMONY: dark minor stabs with a hard semitone rub. Tension held, never released.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 to 7 minutes. The peak of the record by DENSITY, not by lift. It gets more crowded, not brighter. No drop, no breakdown, no release.
NEVER: no euphoric major-key swells, no supersaw leads, no festival build-ups, no anthemic melody, nothing uplifting. This must not sound like EDM at any point.
MIX: dense, warm, physical. Keep the top end restrained and dark.
```

### 07 · FILTRACIÓN — el agua regresando
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, G minor. Title: FILTRACION.
NO VOCALS. No voices of any kind. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: water finding its way back in through the concrete. The pressure easing. Still moving, but loosening.
GROOVE: soft kick. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD but softer now — warm analog bass, rounder, less insistent.
PERCUSSION: wet percussion with reverb, drips and metallic taps between the hand drums. Loose and human.
HARMONY: one minor chord that finally hangs instead of stabbing. Still no resolution.
ARRANGEMENT: 6 minutes. Elements start leaving. Quieter than the track before it and that is the point.
NEVER: no euphoric chords, no supersaw, no builds, no drops, nothing anthemic.
MIX: wet, warm, spacious. Keep the top end restrained and slightly dark.
```

### 08 · SALIDA — se abre la puerta
```
Create an instrumental tech house track. 123 BPM, A minor. Title: SALIDA.
NO VOCALS. No voices of any kind. Purely instrumental.
MOOD: the door opens and it is already daylight. Not triumphant — tired and clear. Coming out of a basement into a street that has been awake for hours without you.
GROOVE: soft kick that fades out in the last third. THE BASSLINE IS THE LEAD until it leaves. Warm analog bass, gentle.
PERCUSSION: it thins out steadily until only shakers and a rim remain.
HARMONY: one warm minor pad holding underneath, fading across the whole track. It never turns major.
ARRANGEMENT: 7 minutes. The comedown. Remove one element at a time. The last two minutes should feel like walking away. End on percussion only, then silence.
NEVER: no euphoric major-key ending, no supersaw, no swell, no lift, no anthemic resolution. It ends tired, not happy.
MIX: warm, soft, distant. Keep the top end restrained and dark.
```

## CÓMO GENERARLAS

1. **Una conversación nueva por rola** en Gemini, para que no se contamine.
2. El botón de descarga → **"Solo audio · Pista musical de MP3"**. Tarda 30-60 s
   en llegar a `~/Downloads` porque se renderiza del lado del servidor — no es
   que falló el clic.
3. **Si alguna trae voz, regenérala.** Es la única regla dura del disco.
4. Déjalas en `~/Downloads` tal cual salgan. Yo mido, mapeo, corrijo, masterizo,
   secuencio y subo — igual que con OFRENDA.

**Ojo con la duración:** Gemini corta en ~2:50 aunque le pidas 6 o 7 minutos.
Ocho rolas dan ~20 min de set. Si quieres una hora, son ~20 rolas — genera más
niveles, no rolas más largas.
